Academics, as the name suggests, is an academic website template for schools, colleges and universities. With a solid tool like Academics, you can now avoid building a page from scratch as the web design is ready pre-defined for you. You can now promote your popular courses and get more students to sign up with a professional and sophisticated academic page. Academics also have a clean and eye-catchy look, which emphasizes your content primarily. Also, due to the cleanness, the experience will always be smooth and flawless. Moreover, Academics is based on Bootstrap Framework to guarantee full flexibility. It also practices all the other modern rules and regulations of web development. Full-width banner, registration form, sticky drop-down menu, social icons and newsletter subscription form are just some of the very many features Academics controls.
